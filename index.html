Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Home Ownership Advisory System.

How does KeyOwn convert my monthly rent into equity?

KeyOwn collaborates with a network of financial co-investment platforms. A structured portion of your rent is co-invested in safe, high-yielding housing debt instruments and real estate investment trusts (REITs). This fractional holding grows steadily and compiles into a substantial down payment fund for your eventual property title transfer.

Is my accumulated equity safe if I decide to move?

Absolutely. Your accumulated co-ownership equity is fully liquid and legally registered in your personal name under our partner trust. If you relocate or choose to exit before the 120-month cycle, you can choose to cash out your fractional holdings at the prevailing market rates or roll over the equity into a new rental property within the HOAS framework.

How does KeyOwn guarantee a lower mortgage rate?

We work directly with major national banks (HDFC, SBI, ICICI) to report rental history as a primary positive credit signal. Flawless rental tracking combined with our proprietary Down Payment Matching trust decreases the lender's risk profile, unlocking a guaranteed lower interest spread (saving you an estimated 0.5% - 0.75% over normal buyers).

Is this a rent-to-own scheme?

No. Standard rent-to-own schemes lock you into a single developer's building, often with heavily inflated prices and severe penalties for late payments. KeyOwn Habitat is a portable advisory and co-ownership framework that lets you build liquid asset equity while renting anywhere, giving you full choice of your ultimate purchase catalog.

What are the eligibility criteria to join?

To join KeyOwn Habitat, you need to be a salaried professional or business owner with a stable income and a clean rental history for at least the past 12 months. We do not require a perfect credit score to start, as our system helps build your credit profile over time.

Are there any hidden fees or advisory charges?

We believe in absolute transparency. There are no upfront hidden fees. KeyOwn charges a flat 1.5% advisory fee only when you successfully transition to homeownership and we facilitate your mortgage. During the rental phase, your entire co-investment portion goes directly into your equity pool.

Can I buy a home before the 10-year transition period?

Yes! The 120-month (10-year) timeline is just a standard projection. Depending on your rent amount, savings rate, and chosen property value, many of our clients accumulate enough equity to make their down payment in as little as 4 to 6 years.
